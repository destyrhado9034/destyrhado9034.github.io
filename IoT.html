Internet of Things

The internet of things, or IoT, is a network of interrelated devices that connect and exchange data with other IoT devices and the cloud. IoT devices are typically embedded with technology such as sensors and software and can include mechanical and digital machines and consumer objects.

Smart Home

A smart home refers to a residence where various electronic devices and systems are connected to a central network, allowing for automation, remote control, and intelligent interaction between the homeowner and their home environment.


Temperature & Humidity Sensor

A temperature and humidity sensor is a device designed to measure and monitor the ambient temperature and relative humidity in a given environment.

Gas Sensor

A gas sensor is a device designed to detect the presence of specific gases in the surrounding environment.
